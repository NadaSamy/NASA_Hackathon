Mental Risks

Relationship issues

Increased strain on relationships with family and friends due to lack of contact.

Stress of extreme isolation and confinement

This can lead to anxiety problems or depression.

Mood disorders

Happens during long deep-space transits, with lack of a visual connection to Earth—even spaceflight can be boring!

Sleep problems

Due to the lack of natural light.

Adverse cognitive or behavioral conditions

Happens if adverse behavioral health conditions are undetected or inadequately mitigated.

Fun Fact

NASA’s evidence report suggests past space missions may have ended early as a result of behavioral problems.
