Physical Risks

Space has no air

Ihis can lead to breathing problems.

Radiation

It is dangerous because it has sufficient energy to change or break DNA molecules, which can damage or kill a cell. This can lead to health problems ranging from acute effects to long term effects.

Decompression Sickness

Decompression sickness (DCS) results when the body is exposed to rapid and significant decreases in atmospheric pressure.

Bone & Muscle Loss

Astronauts lose on average 1% to 2% of their bone mineral density every month. Also, the loss of muscle mass corresponds to the loss of strength which leads to a needed emergency procedure.
